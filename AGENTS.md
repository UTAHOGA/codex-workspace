## Legal / Legislative Drafting Rules

- Maintain formal, neutral, professional language.
- Avoid exaggerated or unsupported claims.
- Separate facts, legal concerns, policy concerns, and requested action.
- Draft for attorney, agency, legislative, or stakeholder review.
- If legal authority is required, flag it for verification instead of inventing it.
