# Legal / Legislative Draft Workflow

Use this workflow for:
- legal letters
- legislative comments
- policy responses
- injunction drafts
- stakeholder notices
- agency-facing explanations

## Task

Draft or revise the requested document.

## Required Structure

1. Purpose
2. Background
3. Key Issues
4. Legal / Procedural Concerns
5. Requested Action
6. Closing Statement

## Rules

- Do not invent citations.
- Do not overstate legal conclusions.
- Use professional advocacy language.
- Keep U.O.G.A. positioned as a coordinating industry association.
- Make the document suitable for attorney or legislative review.

## Final Output

Provide:
1. Clean draft
2. Summary of changes
3. Risk notes
4. Suggested next step
